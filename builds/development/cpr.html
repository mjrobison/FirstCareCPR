CPR & AED Certification

Course Descripion:

Our CPR & AED Certification is designed for the responder. This class teaches the skills necessary to overcome reluctance to act in emergency situations and to recognize and care for life-threatening respiratory or cardiac emergencies. Through “hands-on” training, students will learn the standard protocols for administering proper CPR techniques and understand when and how to use an automated external defibrillator (AED).

Designed for:

Everyday Citizens. Basically anyone who ... (Employees; Parents; Teachers; Coaches; Babysitters and other Care Givers).

Certification Length:

Two Years

Class Length:

Four Hours
