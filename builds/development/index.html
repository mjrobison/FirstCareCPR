First Care CPR

We instruct on the Life saving methods of CPR, AED, & First Aid. Certification is for two years. We teach these classes for the professional and for the average person just wanting to know how to help in an emergency.

We teach First Aid, CPR, AED, & Blood Born Pathogens. We have regular classes scheduled throughout the week and occasionally on Saturday morning. We are willing to travel to businesses or homes to accommodate our students.
